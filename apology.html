I'm So Sorry

A letter from the heart 🌸

I've thought a lot about what happened, and I know that I messed up. There's no excuse for it. None at all.

The truth is, I never want to ruin the special vibe between us by arguing about worthless people. But I made a stupid, terrible decision — and that just made everything so much worse.

My mom — who shares a mutual friend with one of them — pushed me so hard to go to that gathering. They kept changing their plans for me, again and again. All I wanted was to get rid of them without upsetting you. I told myself, "It's just one hour." I didn't really think about what I was doing, or how wrong it was.

I chose the easiest, dumbest, most destructive way out — just so you wouldn't think I was fighting with you over them, or that I really wanted to go. I just wanted to do it once, to get them off my back.

But I really, truly messed up.

I'm so sorry. I've never felt this much regret and guilt in my life. I didn't even know how to apologize in a way that you could accept — a way that could make up for my stupid mistake.

That's why I went and watched front-end tutorials. I worked hard, spent hours learning, and built this website for you — not for any other reason, just to show you how sorry I am and how much you mean to me.

I know a website can't erase the pain. But please know that every line of code, every little heart on this page, comes from a place of deep regret and even deeper love for you. 💗

I am completely ashamed of what I did, and from the bottom of my heart, I'm asking for your forgiveness. I will do whatever it takes to earn your trust back.

What I'm truly sorry for 🌷

😔 Hurting your feelings 😶 Not thinking first 💬 Not communicating better 🤍 Making you feel unheard ⏳ Taking you for granted

With all my love and deepest regret 💝
